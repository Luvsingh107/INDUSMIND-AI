# INDUSCHEM Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd.

## Company Overview

INDUSCHEM Manufacturing Pvt. Ltd. is a medium-scale specialty chemical manufacturing company located in Dahej, Gujarat, India.

The company manufactures industrial chemicals used in pharmaceuticals, water treatment, agrochemicals, and process industries.
